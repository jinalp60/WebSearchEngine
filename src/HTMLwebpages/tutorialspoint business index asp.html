About Us

Tutorials Point originated from the idea that there exists a class of readers who respond better to online content and prefer to learn new skills at their own pace from the comforts of their drawing rooms.

We have grown rapidly with more than 35M+ users using Tutorials point in more than 200 countries.

Our mission is to deliver Simply Easy Learning with clear, crisp, and to-the-point content on a wide range of technical and non-technical subjects without any preconditions and impediments. We have partnered with more than 1000 Global Instructors to come up with a very rich library of video courses and eBooks.
